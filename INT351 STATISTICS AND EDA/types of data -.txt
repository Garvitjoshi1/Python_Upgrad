types of data :-
1. Quantitative(Numeric based) - Discrete, continuous (Continuous data has any value within a given range while discrete data is supposed to have a distinct value)
2. Qualitative(Non-numeric) - Nominal(ordering can't be defined like red is greter then blue or not), 
                              Ordinal(ordered values - like grading system)
3. Categorical Data - Categorical data are used to represent the characteristics. or can also be represented in from of 0 and 1.
4. Time Series Data - It is the collection of a sequence of numbers collected at a regular interval over a certain period of time.
                      ex :- price of stocks
5. Text Data - Text data is nothing but literals.
